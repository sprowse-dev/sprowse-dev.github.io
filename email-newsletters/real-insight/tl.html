3 Interior Design Trends to Look For
 

When working with clients, you're likely to encourage them to keep their homes looking fresh. They can do this by paying attention to what's trendy. If you're curious about what to expect for the upcoming year, pay attention to the following three interior design trends.

Bold marble. If you're looking for a strong focal point in your kitchen, encourage your clients to consider marble. They can even use a bold color such as blue, which will keep the area feeling fun and natural.

Upcycle materials. Have them gain inspiration from special memories by incorporating heirlooms. These items create a calming environment so potential buyers feel at peace.

Wine displays. Previous trends saw homeowners gravitate toward wine cellars, but now people want to show off their bottles. Wine walls add intrigue and almost act like art.

Start planning ahead for your clients in 2023 by thinking about interior design trends for the new year. Breathing new life into a space doesn't need to be expensive, so encourage clients to incorporate these trends into their homes.
